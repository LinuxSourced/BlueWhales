Blue whales are the largest mammals in the world weighing 200 tons(400,000 LBS). A blue whales heart is the size of a small car. A Blue Whale can eat up to four tons a day. They eat large amounts of krill or shrimp like Crustacean. The Blue Whale has many different body parts. Starting from the front to the back of the whale, baleen(whale teeth), Rostrum, throat grooves, eyes, blowhole, ears, dorsal ridges, flippers, umbilicus, flukes, and there uro-genital slit. These giant marine mammals can live from 80 to 90 years of age. Blue whales can be found in the Pacific, Atlantic, and Antarctic Ocean. Typically they live deep in the middle of the Ocean.
